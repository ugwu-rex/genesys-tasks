My Favorite Animal

Dogs are domestic animals, and are often referrd to as man's best friend, this title came from the long relationships of loyalty, companionship and service. Dogs put their life in line to protect their owner. A well trained dog is calm in various situations, follows instructions and respects the owner. In some cases, dogs can be more loyal than some people are to their partner in relationships.
